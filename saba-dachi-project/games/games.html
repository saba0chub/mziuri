HOLLOW KNIGHT

Hollow Knight is a 2D action-adventure game set in a mysterious,
ruined underground kingdom called Hallownest. You play as a small,
silent knight exploring this vast world full of strange creatures, ancient secrets,
and decaying civilizations.

N2

Terraria

Terraria is a popular 2D sandbox game that mixes exploration,
building, crafting, and combat into one big open-ended experience.
At its core, it’s often described as “2D Minecraft,” but it has its own identity
with much deeper combat and progression.

N3

ARC Raiders

ARC Raiders is a multiplayer sci-fi extraction shooter where you scavenge a dangerous
world,fight robots (and sometimes other players), and try to escape with loot.
